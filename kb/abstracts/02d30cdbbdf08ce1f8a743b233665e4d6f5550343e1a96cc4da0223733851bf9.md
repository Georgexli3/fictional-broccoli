MECO Engineering Company, Inc. submitted this Statement of Qualifications to the City of Kirksville, MO in response to a Request for Qualifications for professional engineering services related to Water Treatment Plant Improvements. The submittal outlines MECO's 40-year history of municipal civil engineering, with a specific focus on potable water and wastewater systems. MECO leverages its Kirksville, MO office location — situated approximately 6 blocks from the treatment facility — to offer both short-term and long-term improvement strategies. Short-term services include addressing nonoperational valves, rebuilding service pumps, ensuring proper chemical feeds, and preserving structural piping. Long-term services encompass capital planning, site expansion, water treatment technology selection, and 2–20 year capital depreciation planning. The firm also highlights its NavVis LiDAR 3D scanning capability for rapid as-built documentation of existing facilities, its experience securing federal and state funding (USDA, SRF, CDBG), and its in-house multidisciplinary team covering mechanical/electrical, civil, and water treatment engineering. The proposed project team includes Ryan Huseman PE (Project Manager), Max Middendorf PE (Water Engineering Expert), Kevin Garnett PE (Civil Engineer), Robert Hogan (Civil Engineer), and Andrew Long (Technical Manager).
