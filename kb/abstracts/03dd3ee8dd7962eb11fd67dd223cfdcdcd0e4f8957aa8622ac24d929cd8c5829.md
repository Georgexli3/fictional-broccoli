MECO Engineering Company, Inc. submitted a Statement of Qualifications to the City of Dixon, Missouri, seeking selection as the city's professional engineering consultant and on-call city engineer. The SOQ presents MECO's 40-year history of serving municipalities across Missouri and Illinois, its seven office locations (with the nearest in Jefferson City, MO, approximately 55 miles from Dixon), and its staff of 60 professionals including 13 licensed Professional Engineers, 5 staff engineers, 4 Professional Land Surveyors, and 1 Certified Floodplain Manager. The full range of services offered encompasses land surveying/GIS, site and civil engineering, structural and bridge engineering, water treatment and distribution, sanitary sewer and wastewater treatment, pump/lift/vacuum stations, stormwater hydrology and hydraulics, transportation design, creek bank stabilization, electrical/controls/mechanical engineering, construction administration, right-of-way acquisition, and planning and zoning plan review. MECO also highlights its pre-qualification status with MoDOT, MoDOT LPA, IDOT, and the Illinois Capital Development Board, as well as active corporate licensure in Missouri for both professional engineering and land surveying, and registration to practice in eight states.
