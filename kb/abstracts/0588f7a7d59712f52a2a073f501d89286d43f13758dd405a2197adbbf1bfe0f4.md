MECO Engineering Company, Inc. submitted this Statement of Qualifications to the City of Palmyra, MO on December 1, 2025 (Project No. 001-904), seeking selection as the City's on-call professional engineering consultant. The SOQ presents MECO's full-service civil and municipal engineering capabilities, which span land surveying/GIS, general site civil, structural and bridge engineering, transportation design, water treatment and distribution, pump/lift/vacuum stations, sanitary sewer collection, wastewater treatment, electrical/controls/mechanical engineering, stormwater hydrology/hydraulics, creek bank stabilization, construction administration, right-of-way acquisition, and P&Z plan review. MECO emphasizes its Hannibal, MO office location—approximately 13 miles from Palmyra—as a key logistical advantage, along with its 40-year track record serving Missouri and Illinois municipalities. The firm operates six offices and employs 60 staff including 13 licensed PEs, 4 PLSs, and 1 CFM. MECO is pre-qualified with MoDOT, MoDOT LPA, IDOT, and the Illinois Capital Development Board. Key project examples submitted include regional water system design, water treatment plant improvements, sanitary sewer collection system rehabilitation, wastewater treatment facility design, structural investigations, and bridge engineering work for Marion County—including bridges within Palmyra, MO.
