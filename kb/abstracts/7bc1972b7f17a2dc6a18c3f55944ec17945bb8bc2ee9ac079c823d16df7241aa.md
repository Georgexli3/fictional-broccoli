MECO Engineering Company, Inc. submitted this Statement of Qualifications to the City of Hunnewell in response to a solicitation for Professional Engineering Services related to Wastewater Treatment Improvements. The SOQ presents MECO's 40-year municipal engineering track record, its six-office regional footprint (including a Hannibal, MO office 30 miles from Hunnewell), and deep familiarity with Missouri Department of Natural Resources (MODNR) financing and regulatory requirements. The document outlines MECO's client-centric project approach philosophy, in which the client leads project development rather than the engineer imposing solutions. MECO proposes a four-person specialized project team led by Robert Hogan, PMP (Project Manager), supported by Max Middendorf, PE (Wastewater Expert), Kevin Garnett, PE (Stormwater/Civil), and Andy Long (Technical Manager), with a combined 119 years of experience. The SOQ details relevant prior municipal wastewater experience across Missouri and Illinois communities, demonstrates capacity and QC/QA processes, lists pre-qualifications with MoDOT, IDOT, and Illinois CDB, and provides a reference list of current municipal and institutional clients. Deliverables implicit in the proposed engagement include preliminary engineering reports/facility plans, design, permitting, funding application assistance, construction management, and construction observation.
